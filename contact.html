Frequently Asked Questions

For standard items like cinnamon rolls and artisan breads, 3–5 days notice is usually enough. Decorated cookies and pastries need 1–2 weeks. Custom cakes, especially tiered or heavily decorated designs, require 2–3 weeks minimum. For holidays and weddings, booking early is strongly recommended.
Amy offers both pickup (at her home in Tulsa) and local delivery within the Tulsa metro area. Delivery fees depend on distance and order size. You'll discuss this directly with Amy after placing your order.
No payment is collected online. After you place your order, Amy will reach out to confirm details and share payment options, which typically include Venmo, PayPal, cash, or Zelle. A 50% deposit is required for custom cake orders to secure your date.
Amy does her best to accommodate common dietary needs and can discuss options for gluten-sensitive, nut-free, and dairy-free recipes. Please note that her kitchen is a home kitchen and is not certified allergen-free. Always communicate your needs clearly when placing your order.
